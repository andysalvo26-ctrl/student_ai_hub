# AI News Entry Template

Use this structure for all "AI News That Matters" entries.

---

## Headline

A short, descriptive headline that names the development without editorializing.

**Good:** "OpenAI Releases Updated Guidelines for Academic Use"  
**Avoid:** "Game-Changing AI Rules Could Transform Education!"

---

## Source

Publication name and date.

**Example:** *AP News*, February 3, 2026

---

## What Happened

2-4 sentences. Facts as reported. Synthesize rather than list.

**Do:**
- State what was announced, released, or observed
- Tie facts to business implications where relevant
- Acknowledge contested or unverified claims

**Avoid:**
- Interpretation or opinion
- Lists of companies/products (synthesize instead)
- More than 4 sentences

---

## Why It Matters to Students

2-3 sentences. Relevance to someone studying or entering the workforce.

**Do:**
- Connect to coursework, internships, or career considerations
- Note practical implications
- Stay grounded

**Avoid:**
- Overstating certainty
- Making predictions
- More than 3 sentences

---

## What to Watch Next

1-2 sentences. What's uncertain or might change.

**Do:**
- Acknowledge unknowns
- Note follow-up developments to watch

**Avoid:**
- Predictions
- Advice
- More than 2 sentences

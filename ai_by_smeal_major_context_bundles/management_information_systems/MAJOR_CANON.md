# Management Information Systems Major: Canonical Description

Management Information Systems focuses on technology-supported techniques for exploring, analyzing, integrating, and reporting business data to facilitate fact-based decision-making and enterprise-wide management.

## Core Focus Areas

- Technology-supported techniques for exploring business data
- Business data analysis
- Business data integration
- Business data reporting
- Fact-based decision-making
- Enterprise-wide management
- Business analytics
- Systems analysis and design
- Core business processes

## Common Contexts and Domains

- Enterprise-wide management contexts
- Business data environments
- Decision-making contexts
- Business analytics contexts
- Systems analysis contexts
- Industry, consulting, and government sectors

## Disciplinary Emphasis

Management Information Systems emphasizes technology-supported techniques for exploring, analyzing, integrating, and reporting business data to facilitate fact-based decision-making and enterprise-wide management, developing proficiency in business analytics, systems analysis and design, and core business processes.

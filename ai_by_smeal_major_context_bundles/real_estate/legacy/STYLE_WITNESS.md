## Style Requirements

Match the style of the completed examples:

### Sentence Rhythm
- Use shorter, direct sentences
- Avoid comma chains (sentences with multiple clauses)
- Break complex ideas into multiple sentences
- One main idea per sentence

### Paragraph Depth
- 2-4 sentences per paragraph typical
- Each paragraph has one clear purpose
- Avoid paragraphs covering multiple topics

### Institutional Tone
- Neutral, descriptive language
- Professional, academic voice
- Write for Smeal College of Business website audience
- Avoid conversational or casual tone

### Avoid AI Voice
- No signposting phrases ("more broadly," "it's important to note," "additionally")
- No over-explanation ("In other words," "To put it simply")
- No meta-commentary ("This section will discuss," "As we've seen")
- No repetitive transitions ("However," "Moreover," "Furthermore")
- No conclusions or summary statements

### Content Approach
- Descriptive, not prescriptive
- Focus on what is, not what should be
- No recommendations or advice
- No tool names or vendor mentions

The Student AI Hub is maintained as a student-led resource to support foundational understanding and ethical reflection on artificial intelligence in academic and professional contexts.

### How to Use the Student AI Hub

###### Start with a topic.

Select a section to learn about a specific aspect of artificial intelligence, including foundational concepts, academic use, business applications, and related considerations.

###### Explore the site and its materials.

The Student AI Hub organizes links, summaries, and reference material to help students explore what is currently known about artificial intelligence. Follow sections and pages to access curated information from across the Hub in a structured, topic-based way.

###### Use the Student AI Hub Site Guide

The Site Guide helps you navigate the Hub’s materials by answering questions grounded in the published content. Use it to clarify definitions, compare topics, or understand how different sections relate, without leaving the page.
